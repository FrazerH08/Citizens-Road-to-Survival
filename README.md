About Citizens' Road to Survival
Citizens' Road to Survival is a student project created by a student who is passionate about web development and this is their final major project. This websites raises awareness of what we cannot afford to let the world become.

The website which features news articles, survival tips, and a dashboard for tracking important updates. Makes this a close experience for our users.

We believe that by sharing knowledge and fostering a sense of community, we can help individuals become more resilient and better prepared for any challenges that may arise. Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope you find the information here helpful and empowering.
